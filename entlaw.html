Entertainment Law


 
The entertainment industry is incredibly complex, and there is really no such thing as “Entertainment Law”, like there is Bankruptcy Law, Family Law, or Criminal Law. Professionals in the entertainment industry encounter issues that involve business formation, transactions, intellectual property, employment, regulatory agencies, multimedia, and the list goes on.
 
What is important is selecting an attorney that is experienced in the entertainment industry and dedicated to staying up-to-date with developments in the business of show. Steven Wolfe is just such an attorney, and his experience with contract law and intellectual property law combine with that experience and determination to help you deal with issues that may come up in your creative ventures.
 
Here are some of the areas that Wolfe Legal Services can offer assistance with:
 
       Drafting and reviewing any variety of music, film, literary, television, or fashion agreements
       Copyright registration, counseling, and portfolio management
       Licensing strategies, negotiations, and agreements
       Publishing and royalties transactions
       Music employment agreements, including union contracts, side musician deals, and band agreements
       Band or business trademarks
       Production agreements
       Management or booking agreements
       Song/collaboration agreements and split sheets
       End-user licenses and agreements
       Litigation strategies
                Copyright infringement
                Breach of contract
                Anything else that affects your goals as an artist
       
       ... and much more.
