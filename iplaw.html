Intellectual Property Law


Whether it be an invention that will make the world a better place, or a unique photograph that captures the magic of our world, or you are starting a business and want to protect your unique identity and commercial standing, Wolfe Legal Services can help.
 
Intellectual property is a unique area of the law, and you need someone who understands the specific nuances of intellectual property law when trying to protect your idea. Steven Wolfe is well-versed in all areas of intellectual property law. As an artist and musician himself, copyright law has been an interest since before the law itself appealed to him. He has experience registering for, negotiating licensing of, and litigating trademark rights. He is also eligible to practice before the United States Patent and Trademark Office as a registered patent attorney and has experience in all matters of patent prosecution and litigation.
 
Intellectual property law can basically be broken down into four major categories:
 
     Copyrights
     Trademarks
     Patents
     Trade Secrets 

​These four areas can all encompass a wide variety of needs and issues that you may face when trying to protect the non-concrete and often enigmatic property that originates from your mind. From registration to litigation and all points in between, Wolfe Legal Services can help guide you through the frequently obscure and ever-changing landscape of intellectual property law.
